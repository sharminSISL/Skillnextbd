About SkillNext BD

SkillNext BD is a virtual training academy established in 2018, dedicated to empowering professionals in the fields of Human Resource Management, Workplace Compliance, and Industrial Relations. We specialize in delivering practical education based on Bangladesh Labour Laws, Employment Rules, and relevant global standards, with an emphasis on accessibility and clarity — proudly offering Bangla-first content tailored to the local workforce. From grievance management to audit readiness, our programs are designed to unlock career-building skills and promote ethical, legally sound workplace practices.
Whether you're an HR practitioner, compliance officer, or factory manager, SkillNext BD is here to help you lead with confidence and competence.

  • Professional Training Programs – Specialized training on HR, payroll, compliance, and employee welfare.
  • Master Classes & Workshops – Practical sessions like “Recruitment to Separation” covering end-to-end HR processes.
  • Labour Law Guidance – Training and consultation on Bangladesh labour laws and compliance.
  • Payroll & HR Operations Training – Hands-on learning for managing payroll and HR systems effectively.
  • Employee Development Programs – Courses designed to enhance skills and career growth.
